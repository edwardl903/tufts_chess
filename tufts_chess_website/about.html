Tufts Chess Club<

The Tufts Chess Club< application enables the chess-playing members of the Tufts Community to have a forum to connect with fellow chess enthusiasts from the Tufts community. Enrolling with Tufts Chess Club< gives you a chance to take part in online matches and improve your skills, take part in the various tournaments that will be conducted, and also be part of a thriving chess community.

Tufts Chess Club

The Tufts Chess Club aims to provide any members of the Tufts Community wishing to play chess with a forum in which to do so. It also aims to teach a basic understanding of the rules of chess to beginning players as well as critical analysis of the tactics of the game to more advanced players. To facilitate the exchange of information among chess-playing members of the Tufts Community so that anyone wishing to do so may find an opponent to play and to improve their skills outside of meetings of the Tufts Chess Club as well as at meetings.
